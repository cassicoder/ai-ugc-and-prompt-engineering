# 04.01 — TOF / MOF / BOF Funnel Rules

TikTok Shop content is structured into three funnel stages, each with its own length, tone, and compliance rules.

---

## TOF — Top of Funnel (15-30s)

**Goal:** Hook scrollers who don't know they want the product yet.

**Length:** 15-30 seconds (15s for product UGC default).

**Rules:**
- ❌ NO dollar amounts
- ❌ NO discount percentages
- ❌ NO hard CTAs ("buy now", "click here")
- ✅ Soft availability framing ("huge sale," "before it's gone")
- ✅ Soft CTAs ("click the orange cart below if you want it")
- ✅ Lifestyle / discovery vibe
- ✅ Curiosity-driven hooks

**The TOF script template (15s, ~42 words):**

```
[HOOK — scroll-stopping open]. [PROBLEM or IDENTITY anchor — 1 sentence].
[3 SPECIFIC FEATURES, comma-separated]. [GENUINE REACTION].
Huge sale right now, click the OR-anj cart below.
```

**Worked example (Adirondack chair):**

> "Okay STOP — y'all need to see this chair right now. It folds, it's real acacia wood, and it holds like four hundred pounds. I've been looking for one for my patio for months and finally found it. Huge sale right now, click the OR-anj cart below before they sell out."

---

## MOF — Middle of Funnel (30-60s)

**Goal:** Convert warm viewers who've already seen TOF content. They know the product exists; now they need details and proof.

**Length:** 30-60 seconds.

**Rules:**
- ✅ Dollar amounts allowed (state real price)
- ✅ Specific value framing ("this would normally be X, on sale for Y")
- ✅ Demo content (show how it works)
- ✅ Medium-strength CTA ("link's in my orange cart below")
- ❌ Still no medical/health claims

**Template:**

```
[HOOK]. [PROBLEM statement]. [DEMO of the product solving the problem]. [SPECIFIC PRICE statement]. [CTA].
```

**Example (Curvy Moon Teeth Cleaner MOF):**

> "Pause — this little thing has changed how my teeth feel. I used to dread my morning routine. Watch this — five modes, LED light at the tip, fully waterproof. Plug it in once a week and it lasts. It's normally over $40 and right now it's under $15. Tap the orange cart below if you want one."

---

## BOF — Bottom of Funnel (60-120s)

**Goal:** Close warm-to-hot leads who've engaged multiple times. They're price-comparing.

**Length:** 60-120 seconds.

**Rules:**
- ✅ Hard scarcity ("only X left," "sale ends tonight" — but only if true)
- ✅ Stack of features and benefits
- ✅ Direct CTAs ("buy now," "tap the cart RIGHT NOW")
- ✅ Triple-fire CTA (CTA at 30s, 60s, end)
- ❌ Still no medical/legal/false claims

**Template:**

```
[HOOK]. [PROBLEM]. [DEMO]. [SOCIAL PROOF or COMPARISON]. [URGENCY].
[CTA 1 at 30s]. [MORE FEATURES]. [CTA 2 at 60s]. [FINAL CTA].
```

---

## Compliance rules across all stages

### Forbidden (CHR violations)
- ❌ Medical claims ("cures," "treats," "FDA approved")
- ❌ Doctor / dentist / professional disparagement ("my dentist couldn't do this")
- ❌ Whole-room utility overclaims ("heats the whole room") for accent products
- ❌ Specific competitor names by brand (HomeGoods, Costco, Wayfair, Amazon)
- ❌ False scarcity ("only 3 left" when there are 300)
- ❌ Before/after weight loss claims
- ❌ Misleading product comparisons

### Required
- ✅ AI label toggled ON for all AI-generated videos
- ✅ Spec claims match the actual listing (see `01-accuracy-protocol.md`)
- ✅ CTAs in-app (orange cart, not external links)

---

## When to use which funnel stage

| Situation | Use |
|---|---|
| Brand new product, cold audience | TOF |
| Mid-funnel retargeting | MOF |
| Sale ending, scarcity moment | BOF |
| First post in a campaign | TOF |
| Closing post on a campaign | BOF |
| Recurring evergreen content | TOF |

For Isaac's current workflow, TOF is the primary mode. The 22 products in this session were all TOF.

---

## Banned hooks (overused / signal "ad")

- "Let me show you..."
- "Today I'm going to..."
- "Hey guys, look at this..."
- "If you're in the market for..."
- "Are you tired of..."
- "Have you been looking for..."

These read as scripted ads, not authentic discovery.

---

## Authentic hook formulas (use these)

### Surprise-praise
"Yo whoever priced this needs a raise."
"I cannot BELIEVE this is the price."

### Curiosity loop
"Tell me why nobody talks about this."
"Why is nobody buying this right now?"

### Stop command
"Okay STOP — you need to see this."
"Wait wait wait —"

### Identity hook
"If you're the type of person who..."
"Pov: you finally found the [product] that..."

### Reaction trigger
"Stop scrolling for a sec."
"Pause —"

## The "Cashvertising lever" stack

Apply these psychological levers from Cashvertising to amplify TOF scripts:

1. **Mental Movies** — paint vivid usage scenarios ("imagine yourself sitting in this on your patio")
2. **Ego Morphing** — viewer becomes the presenter ("when you walk in, the first thing people see...")
3. **Extreme Specificity** — concrete details ("450 pounds, three drawers, six wheels")
4. **Peripheral Route** — emotions/vibes > logic ("makes the room feel like a vacation rental")
5. **LF8 Desires** — survival, enjoyment, freedom from fear, sex, comfortable living, superiority, protection of loved ones, social approval

For TOF, lean Mental Movies + Peripheral Route. For BOF, lean Extreme Specificity.

## Caption + text style guide

- **No periods** at the end of captions or text
- **Lowercase** preferred (mirrors how Gen Z types)
- **Em dashes** allowed for rhythm (` — `)
- **Emojis at end** of phrase, not mid-phrase
- **No hashtags** in TOF captions (signals "branded content")
